# Product Requirement Document

## 01 — Problem Statement

Josh Morden is a Business Analytics and Information Systems student at the University of Iowa. While a resume lists skills and past work, it is a static document that cannot demonstrate the live execution, deployment, and management of a digital product. This landing page solves that problem by serving as a functional, live example of Josh’s ability to build a professional digital presence that integrates data-driven insights with product management principles for a general professional audience.

The target audience includes recruiters, hiring managers, and peer data professionals across various industries. These visitors are looking for a candidate who can bridge the gap between technical data analysis (SQL, Python, Azure) and product delivery. They expect to see a clear value proposition and evidence of technical competence within seconds of arriving on the page.
